How To Play

Unscramble 10 words in Twistle!

Game Rules:

  • Unscramble each jumbled word
  • You have 3 attempts per word
  • 30 seconds time limit for each word
  • Words get progressively longer and more difficult
  • A category hint appears after 10 seconds

Scoring:

  • Base points (10) for solving each word
  • Time bonus: 2 points for each second remaining
  • Attempt bonus: 5 points for each unused attempt
  • Word length bonus: Harder words are worth more
  • No points if you fail to solve a word

Example:

Jumbled: act

Solution: cat

A new set of Twistle words is available each day, with a special theme connecting all the words!

Tips for Success:

  • Look for common letter patterns
  • Pay attention to the word length
  • Use the category hint if you're stuck
  • Try different arrangements of vowels and consonants
